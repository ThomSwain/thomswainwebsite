Thom Swain

A Melbourne-based session musician, educator, and tech-creative.

Thom Swain in an established bassist, guitarist, and music educator. Thom regularly performs and records with a variety of artists in Melbourne and around Australia. He has an active interest in music-technology and interactive media and is currently working towards completing his Bachelor of Computer Science to accompany his Bachelor of Music. Thom is available for session work and private teaching through the email form below.
